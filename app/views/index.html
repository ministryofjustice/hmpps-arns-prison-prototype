Prison pathway team

We are working to design a version of the Strengths and Needs (SAN) assessment specifically for people in custody. This work is part of the wider Assessing Risks, Needs and Strengths (ARNS) programme, which aims to replace OASys with a more usable service.

The prototypes below build on learning from the Community version of SAN and reflect ongoing iteration based on user feedback and prison-specific needs.
